What is Six Hands?

Six Hands is a Japanese-Australian salad brand redefining what it means to eat well. We make salads for people who don’t usually eat salads.

Where is Six Hands located?

Our flagship store is located at Amoy Street, with other outlets opening soon.

Is Six Hands available for franchise?

Yes, Six Hands is currently open to franchise interest in Asia. Please contact us through our website for more information.
